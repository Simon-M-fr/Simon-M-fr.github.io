I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the LEDA laboratory at Université Paris Dauphine - PSL and at the Department of Economics of the University of Copenhagen under a joint agreement. I am writing my thesis under the supervision of Gabrielle Fack and Niels Johannesen.

My research interests are in public economics, corporate finance, and the economics of innovation and science. My work uses large-scale administrative data to study firm behavior, corporate philanthropy, and the role of private funding.

I visited UC Berkeley in Fall 2025, hosted by Matilde Bombardini.

Feel free to contact me for any comments or questions at: simon.muchardt@dauphine.psl.eu or sm@econ.ku.dk.
